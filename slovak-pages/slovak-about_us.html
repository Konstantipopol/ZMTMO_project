Who am I?

My name is Konstantin Negrutsak. I was born in 2003 in Russia, St. Petersburg.
Since childhood I liked math and IT. Till this moment these subjects are my fovorite ones.
When I turned 14 I began to learn python.
However, I was too scared of doing some real projects because of exams and all my homework.
Later I focused on studying in school and played computer games during all free time I had.
Living like this I passed all my school exams and got exellent grades at all subjects and later decided to enter STU in Bratislava. I successfully passed entering exams here and now I'm studying at the FIIT here.
This website is my semester project for ZMTMO. Hope you enjoy it!
